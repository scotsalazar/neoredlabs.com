About Neo Redlabs

Bringing ideas to life through research, innovation and collaboration.

Our Story

Neo Redlabs is a forward‑thinking technology company headquartered in Makati City, Metro Manila. Our mission is to harness the power of digital innovation to drive meaningful change for organisations across industries. From research and development to agile project delivery, we build solutions that help our partners thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

We believe in continuous innovation. Research and development (R&D) comprises systematic activities that companies undertake to innovate and create new products or improve existing ones, helping organisations stay ahead of the competition. Our dedicated teams explore emerging technologies, experiment with prototypes and translate ideas into reliable products.

At the same time, our digital transformation initiatives reimagine how businesses operate and engage with customers. Digital transformation involves using digital technologies to create or modify business processes, culture and customer experiences to meet changing market requirements. We help clients modernise workflows, deliver seamless customer experiences and build resilient, scalable systems.

We’re proud to contribute to the vibrant tech community in the Philippines. Our diverse team brings together engineers, designers, product strategists and subject‑matter experts united by a passion for excellence and a commitment to making a positive impact.

Our Values

Innovation

We cultivate curiosity and encourage experimentation. By investing in R&D, we build original solutions that push the boundaries of what’s possible.

Customer‑Centricity

Our work begins and ends with the customer. We design technology that improves real experiences and meets the needs of a changing market.

Collaboration

Great things happen when people work together. We foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives drive better outcomes.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency and respect. Trust is the foundation of all our relationships – with clients, partners and each other.
