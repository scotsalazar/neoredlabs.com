Executive Assistant / Project & HR Coordinator

We are looking for a highly organized and proactive Executive Assistant / Project & HR Coordinator to support the CEO and assist with company operations. This role combines executive assistance, project coordination, and HR support, ensuring smooth day-to-day operations and efficient project execution.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Serve as the primary point of contact between the CEO, staff, and external partners.
  • Coordinate and track multiple ongoing projects to ensure timely delivery.
  • Handle HR-related tasks such as recruitment, onboarding, and basic employee coordination.
  • Schedule meetings, prepare reports, and maintain organized documentation.
  • Assist in managing company events, communications, and operational workflows.
  • Ensure professional communication and follow-ups with internal and external stakeholders.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, HR, Management, or a related field.
  • 2–3 years of experience in executive assistance, project coordination, or HR.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Proficient in Google Workspace / MS Office and comfortable with online project tools.
  • Able to handle confidential information with discretion.

Nice to Have:

  • Experience in startups, IT, or project-based teams.
  • Ability to adapt to a fast-paced and evolving work environment.

Core Banking Software Engineer

We are seeking a Core Banking Software Engineer to develop, enhance, and maintain our banking systems and applications. This role involves implementing solutions, integrating with financial services, and optimizing system performance to support business operations.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain core banking modules and applications.
  • Implement system enhancements, custom scripts, and workflows.
  • Integrate third-party financial services and APIs.
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve system or performance issues.
  • Collaborate with the business and technical teams to deliver new features.
  • Ensure security and compliance with banking standards.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field.
  • Proficient in at least one programming/scripting language (PL/SQL, Java, or similar).
  • Experience in financial/banking systems or fintech projects preferred.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work in a hybrid setup and meet deadlines with minimal supervision.

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with core banking platforms or financial system integrations.
  • Knowledge of database management and API-based integrations.
